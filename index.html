Viper Arcade

ViPER GAMING: not the only thing that gives you wood behind a desktop

ViPER INDUSTRIES is a world-leading player in both professional gaming equipment and arcade gaming. We're specialized in high-end gaming equipment and giving it a taste of nature. We're bringing an innovative twist to arcade gaming. ViPER INDUSTRIES pushes innovation, design and performance.


History

ViPER INDUSTRIES started in 2000 in Eindhoven, Netherlands as a student project on innovating arcade gaming. Since then we have have expanded to the rest of Europe. In 2012 our second headquarters was erected in Kobenhavn, Denmark, for our gaming equipment.
